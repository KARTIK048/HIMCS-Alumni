About Us

The HIMCS Alumni Association is a vibrant community connecting graduates from the Hindustan Institute of Management and Computer Studies. Our mission is to foster lifelong connections among alumni and between alumni and the institution.

Through events, mentorship programs, and networking opportunities, we aim to empower our members and create meaningful collaborations that benefit students, alumni, and society.

Whether you graduated recently or decades ago, you are an essential part of the HIMCS legacy. Let’s continue building bridges and celebrating the success of our alumni family.
